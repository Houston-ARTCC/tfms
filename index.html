ZHU TFMS

Tool Info

What this tool does:
  • Displays all airborne flights currently inside a ZHU sector (based on position and altitude)
  • Displays flights within 50 NM of the ZHU perimeter if their heading is within 45° to the closest point on the ZHU border
  • Assigns flights to sectors and specialties using official sector polygons and standard specialties
  • Projects specialty loads at +5, +10, +20 minutes (assuming constant heading and groundspeed)
  • Color codes projections to quickly spot trending saturation levels
  • Auto-refreshes every 60 seconds
Filters in effect:
  • Only flights with groundspeed > 20 knots are shown
  • Flights below 10,000 ft are shown as "TRACON"
  • Flights inside ZHU sectors are always shown
Limitations:
  • Sector assignment is based on position and altitude only; does not account for dynamic splits
  • Projections assume constant heading and groundspeed; advanced flight path projection is not modeled
  • Load accuracy decreases as projection time increases
  • Other limitations may exist
